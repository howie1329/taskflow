# ADR-0001: v1 Scope

Date: 2026-01-31
Status: Accepted

## Decision

v1 focuses on the solo workplace loop:

- Inbox (capture/triage/convert)
- Tasks, Projects, Notes
- Schedule (assign-to-day)
- Search/command palette
- Notifications (in-app)
- AI chat (persisted; non-streaming default)

Out of scope for v1:

- mentions/backlinks
- reminders
- recurring tasks
- attachments
- semantic search / embeddings
- research + daily digest
- external calendar sync
- collaboration

## Why

This reduces scope and dependency complexity and targets the missing "make it real" gaps from the legacy app.
